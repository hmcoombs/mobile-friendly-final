Are Pallets Safe to Use?


In the past, pallets were treated with chemicals to make them last longer. Newer pallets are no longer treated with those chemicals. But there’s something else to think about. ick factor”. What was transported on the pallets that you’re using? Was it a dangerous chemical that may have leaked onto the pallet? Was it in a warehouse with mice or rats?

I make sure the pallets are safe by first, only going to companies or businesses that transport or recieve pallets that were not used for chemicals or other hazardious materials.
